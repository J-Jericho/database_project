About Us

Here are just some core values we believe in:

  • Intellectual freedom: this library supports the right of individuals to seek and receive information on all subjects without restriction or censorship.
  • Access to information: this library provides access to information and resources for all members of the community regardless of age, race, gender, or economic status.
  • Lifelong learning: this library promotes learning and education for all ages and stages of life.
  • Cultural awareness: this library recognizes and celebrates the diversity of our community and provide resources and programming that reflect the cultural and linguistic diversity of our users.
  • Privacy and confidentiality: this library protects the privacy of our users and ensures that their personal information and reading choices are kept confidential.
  • Community service: this library is an active and engaged member of the community, providing resources, programming, and support to promote the well-being of our users.
  • Ethical conduct: this library adheres to ethical standards and professional conduct in all of its operations and interactions with our users and the community.
  • Sustainability: this library strives to be environmentally sustainable and promote responsible use of resources.
  • Innovation: this library embraces new technologies and innovative practices to improve access to information and services for our users.
